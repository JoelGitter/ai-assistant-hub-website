üîê Our Multi-Layer Security Approach

We implement a comprehensive security strategy that protects your data at every level:

1. End-to-End Encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard TLS 1.3 encryption. This ensures that even if data is intercepted, it cannot be read or modified.

2. Secure Data Processing

AI processing happens in secure, isolated environments with strict access controls. Our systems are designed to process data without storing sensitive information permanently.

3. Regular Security Audits

We conduct regular security assessments, penetration testing, and vulnerability scans to identify and address potential security issues before they can be exploited.

4. Compliance with Standards

Our systems are designed to meet or exceed industry security standards including SOC 2, GDPR, and CCPA requirements for data protection and privacy.
