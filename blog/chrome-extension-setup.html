üöÄ Quick Start: Install in 3 Steps

Getting started with AI Assistant Hub is incredibly simple. Here's the fastest way to get up and running:

Step 1: Install from Chrome Web Store

The easiest way to install AI Assistant Hub is directly from the Chrome Web Store:

  1. Click the Install Extension button
  2. Click "Add to Chrome" in the Chrome Web Store
  3. Confirm the installation when prompted

üí° Pro Tip

Make sure you're using Google Chrome or a Chromium-based browser (Edge, Brave, etc.) for the best compatibility and performance.

Step 2: Pin the Extension

After installation, pin the extension to your toolbar for quick access:

  1. Click the puzzle piece icon in your Chrome toolbar
  2. Find "AI Assistant Hub" in the list
  3. Click the pin icon to keep it visible

Step 3: Create Your Account

Set up your account to unlock all features:

  1. Click the AI Assistant Hub icon in your toolbar
  2. Click "Sign Up" or "Create Account"
  3. Enter your email and create a password
  4. Verify your email address

üéØ First-Time Setup Walkthrough

Let's walk through your first use of AI Assistant Hub:

Testing Form Filling

Navigate to any website with a form (like a contact form or signup page):

  1. Click the AI Assistant Hub icon in your toolbar
  2. Select "Smart Form Filling" from the menu
  3. Choose your preferred formality level
  4. Watch as the AI intelligently fills the form
  5. Review and adjust any suggestions as needed

Testing Page Summarization

Visit any content-rich webpage to test summarization:

  1. Click the AI Assistant Hub icon
  2. Select "Page Summarization"
  3. Choose summary length (Brief, Detailed, or Bullet Points)
  4. Review the AI-generated summary
  5. Use the summary for quick understanding or note-taking
