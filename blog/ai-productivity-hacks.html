Before diving into specific hacks, understand these fundamental principles:

1. Automate the Repetitive

Identify tasks you do repeatedly and automate them first. This includes form filling, data entry, content summarization, and routine communications. AI excels at these predictable, pattern-based activities.

AI tools get better with use. Regularly review and refine your prompts, templates, and workflows to continuously improve your productivity gains.
