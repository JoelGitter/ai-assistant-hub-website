Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI Assistant Hub

How does the AI form filling work?

Our AI analyzes the form context and field labels to generate appropriate responses. You can choose from different formality levels (Professional, Casual, Friendly) to match your needs.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and don't store your personal information permanently. Page content is processed but not saved. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely! You can cancel your Pro subscription anytime through your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

What websites does it work on?

AI Assistant Hub works on any website with forms or content. It's compatible with all major browsers and web applications.

How accurate are the AI suggestions?

Our AI provides highly accurate suggestions based on context, but you should always review and edit the content before submitting important forms.
